About BuildSpace
What is BuildSpace?
BuildSpace is a social network for people who enjoy and are interested in smaller-scale computer and technology projects and solutions. This platform makes it easy to share your personal passion for technology with others. Make friends with people from around the world and see what kind of projects they are working on or have completed. BuildSpace aims to connect passionate people and create a friendly community for everyone.
Who made BuildSpace?
BuildSpace was the creative project of Jacob Walker while he was in college at BYU. It all started as a creative project in his web programming class. Jacob is a PC enthusiast. After building his own computer for the first time, he wanted a way to share this with other people who liked computers. He saw the need for a public space for enthusiasts to gather, and BuildSpace was born. BuildSpace strives to be a fun and friendly way to learn, share, and enjoy personal computer projects.
